Guac.

This is so easy and so good. It's always the hit of the party and it's gone before anything else on the table.

  • 1 Avocado
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Tomato
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Cup Cilantro
  1. Dice up Onion, Tomato, and Cilantro
  2. Peel and mash avocado
  3. Add onion, tomato, cilantro, and lemon juice into avocado
